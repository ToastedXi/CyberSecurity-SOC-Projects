## Key Components Learned

The course provided hands on, actionable knowledge across the following core areas:

- **Phishing Fundamentals**  
  Definition, mechanics, and psychological techniques used in phishing attacks.

- **Types of Phishing Attacks**  
  Comprehensive coverage of email phishing, smishing (SMS), vishing (voice), and social media-based attacks.

- **Real World Attack Analysis**  
  Breakdown of sophisticated attacker tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) with real case studies.

- **Detection Best Practices**  
  Identifying red flags in emails, URLs, domains, attachments, and sender behavior.

- **Incident Reporting & Response**  
  Proper procedures for reporting suspicious activity and supporting effective incident response workflows.

- **Prevention Strategies**  
  Implementing technical controls, multi-layered defenses, security tools, and employee awareness training.
